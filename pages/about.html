About us

Keshav Memorial Engineering College (KMEC) is the latest engineering institute to be launched by Keshav Memorial Technical Educational Society with the sole purpose of promoting quality engineering education. The present Executive team comprises of luminaries having vast experience in different fields from legal to education. KMEC is a sister concern of KMIT and NGIT. Therefore, all the facilities and successful teaching-learning processes of KMIT and NGIT will be implemented in this college as well. KMEC is approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, and is affiliated to Osmania University (O.U), Hyderabad and recognized by Govt. of Telangana. KMEC is co-promoted and powered by GENESIS SOLUTIONS PVT LTD, a premier and pioneer institute in Hyderabad imparting industry focused software training and education in emerging technologies and having tie-ups with leading MNCs.
